About Nuseum of Tanta Mien

Nyoo'zēǝm

The name Nuseum draws inspiration from the word "museum," symbolizing a space that preserves and celebrates art, heritage, and human creativity. At Nuseum of Tanta Mien, we honor the legacy of Ny. Mien, a remarkable woman of Chinese descent whose life was a tapestry of compassion, cultural harmony, and artistic expression.

Our café is more than a place to enjoy exceptional coffee and cuisine—it's a tribute to Ny. Mien’s vision of fostering community through good deeds and shared stories. Join us to experience the warmth of her spirit in every cup and bite.

The Legacy of Ny. Mien

Ny. Mien, a beloved merchant with roots in Chinese heritage, lived a life of profound kindness and cultural connection. Having resided in Minahasa and Tagulandang, she embodied grace and wisdom, blending traditions with an open heart. Her ability to cherish all living beings and nature itself set her apart, inspiring those around her.

Her stories, filled with lessons of ethics and compassion, are preserved at @nuseumoftantamien on our second floor—a space dedicated to her legacy. Visit us to hear tales of her vibrant life, from her stylish flair to her timeless wisdom: “Good habits start small and grow with you.” With a nod to her charm, we say #KecupBibirSmengken, celebrating Oma Mien’s enduring love and vitality.
